About Us

We are Community of Ignited Minds of Youth

"What the country needs is what we aim for"

Our purpose is to connect the youth of India and provide them with an open platform where they can discuss important topics, interact with each other, collaborate with everyone else, and grow together. People here share their ideas, knowledge, and experiences. Humans learn and get better when they learn collaboratively in a supportive environment.
